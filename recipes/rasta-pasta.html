Rasta Pasta Recipe

Description

Rasta pasta is a colorful and flavorful pasta dish made with bell peppers, onions, and jerk seasoning.

Indgredients

  • 1 lb pasta
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup onions
  • 1/4 cup jerk seasoning
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add bell peppers and onions to the pan and saut√© until soft.
  4. Stir in jerk seasoning and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add heavy cream to the pan and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Combine cooked pasta with the sauce.
  7. Serve with grated cheese on top.
