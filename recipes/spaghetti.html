Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a popular Italian dish made with pasta and tomato sauce.

Indgredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Grated cheese
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add spaghetti pasta to the boiling water and cook until tender.
  3. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  4. In a separate pan, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned.
  5. Add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the pan.
  6. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce.
  8. Serve with grated cheese on top.
