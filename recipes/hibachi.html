Hibachi

Description

Beef and chicken hibachi are popular Japanese dishes, typically made by grilling marinated meat on a flat, high-heat grill.

Indgredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup minced ginger
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1/4 cup minced lemon
  • 1/4 cup minced lime
  • 1/4 cup minced oregano
  • 1/4 cup minced chive
  • 1/4 cup minced shallot
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic chive
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic scallion
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic chive

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Marinate meat for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Grill meat on a flat, high-heat grill.
